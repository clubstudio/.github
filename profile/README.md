# Club Studio

**A purpose-first brand studio helping ambitious businesses connect with their audience and inspire them into action.**

We're a brand strategy, web design, and software development studio based in Altrincham, Greater Manchester. Since 2013, we've been helping businesses discover their purpose and use it as the foundation for everything — from brand identity to marketing websites to custom software.

---

### What We Do

**Brand Strategy** — We help businesses rediscover their *why*. Through our Purpose Discovery Workshop and Brand Foundations process, we define your purpose, vision, values, audience, positioning, and voice — giving you a clear plan from where you are to where you want to be.

**Marketing Websites** — Content-led, conversion-focused websites built on [Craft CMS](https://craftcms.com/). We start with what needs to be said, then design and develop around it. Every site includes discovery, content strategy, UX/UI design, development, and ongoing support.

**Custom Software** — Bespoke web applications built with [Laravel](https://laravel.com/) to automate processes, streamline workflows, and deliver a consistent brand experience. Tailored to how your business actually works.

---

### Our Tech Stack

We have extensive experience working across the full stack, including **PHP**, **Laravel**, **Craft CMS**, **Vue.js**, **Inertia.js**, **Alpine.js**, **MySQL**, **Vite**, and **Twig**. Whether it's a content-managed marketing site or a complex bespoke application, we choose the right tools for the job and deliver robust, maintainable solutions.
